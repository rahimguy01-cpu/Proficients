FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know

Do I need technical knowledge to start?
+
Absolutely not. Proficients handles 100% of the technical work — installation, configuration, management and maintenance. You just own the business and collect the income.
How much can I earn from a WiFi business?
+
Earnings depend on your location, customer traffic and setup size. A well-placed hotspot in a busy area can generate consistent monthly income. We'll give you a realistic estimate during your site inspection.
What does the setup process look like?
+
You apply → we visit your location → we create a plan → we install and configure everything → your hotspot goes live. From application to launch typically takes a few days.
What locations work best for a WiFi hotspot?
+
High foot traffic areas work best — shops, markets, salons, restaurants, waiting areas, schools and residential compounds. Our team will assess your specific location during the site visit.
Do you manage the business after setup?
+
Yes. Proficients provides ongoing management — monitoring uptime, managing users, handling technical issues and ensuring your hotspot runs smoothly at all times.
How do I get started?
+
Simply tap Apply below or message us on WhatsApp. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours to discuss your goals and next steps.
Where do you operate in Uganda?
+
We are based in Namasuba, Opposite Freedom City, Kampala and currently serve clients across the greater Kampala area. Contact us to check availability in your specific location.
